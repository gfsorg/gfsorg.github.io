About

What we are and why we exist

GFSO is a community-driven organization dedicated to creating, maintaining, and promoting free and open-source software for everyone. We believe software should be transparent, accessible, and built collaboratively — not locked behind proprietary walls.

Our projects span a wide range of domains: developer tooling, utilities, libraries, and full applications — all released under permissive or copyleft licenses. Whether you are a seasoned contributor or a first-timer, there is a place for you here.

Principles

The values that guide every project we ship

Freedom

Users have the right to run, study, share, and modify any GFSO project without restriction.

Transparency

Every line of code, every decision, and every roadmap item is public by default.

Simplicity

We favour focused, well-documented tools over bloated, opaque systems.

Community

Maintainers are stewards, not gatekeepers. Contributions are welcomed and nurtured.
